# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: January 29, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to "Class Check-in Assistant" (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

## App Overview

Class Check-in Assistant is a course management and student attendance tracking tool designed to help teachers and training institutions manage courses and student attendance records.

## Information Collection and Use

### Information We Collect

The App only collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

- **Course Information**: Course names, total class hours, used class hours, and other course-related data
- **Student Information**: Student names, contact information, and other basic details
- **Check-in Records**: Student attendance time and records

### Data Storage Method

**All data is stored in a local SQLite database on your device** and is never uploaded to any external servers or cloud services. This means:

- Your data is completely stored on your device
- We cannot access any of your data
- Data is not shared with any third parties
- There are no network transmissions or external requests

### Purpose of Data Use

The collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

- Displaying and managing courses you create
- Recording and querying student check-in records
- Providing course statistics and calendar view features
- Improving the app user experience

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- Data security depends on your device's security settings
- We recommend regularly backing up your device data
- Uninstalling the app will delete all local data
- We do not provide cloud backup or recovery services

## Permissions

The App may require the following device permissions:

- **Storage Permission**: To store database files locally on your device
- **Calendar Permission** (if applicable): To display course calendar views

We do not request any unnecessary permissions. All permissions are used solely for core app functionality.

## Third-Party Services

The App **does not use any third-party analytics services, advertising services, or other third-party SDKs**. Your data is not shared with any third parties.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is primarily intended for use by teachers and training institutions.

## Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

- Deleting specific courses or student records within the app
- Uninstalling the app, which will delete all locally stored data

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated through app updates. We recommend reviewing this policy regularly to stay informed.

## Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

- **Right of Access**: You can view all your data within the app at any time
- **Right to Deletion**: You can delete your data at any time
- **Right to Data Portability**: Since data is stored locally, you can export data through device backup features

## Data Retention

Data will remain on your device until you actively delete it or uninstall the app.
